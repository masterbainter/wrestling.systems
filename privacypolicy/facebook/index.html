Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how wrestling.systems (referred to as "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website and its services (the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

a) Information You Provide to Us

When you create an account and log in using the Facebook Login service, we collect the following personal information from your Facebook profile:

  • Name: To personalize your experience.
  • Email Address: To communicate with you regarding your account and our Services.
  • Facebook Profile Information: This includes your unique Facebook ID and public profile picture. We use this to establish and manage your account.

b) Information We Collect Automatically

When you access our Services, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • Log Data: Like most websites, our servers automatically record information, including your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, operating system, and the date and time of your access.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to operate and administer our Services. A cookie is a piece of information stored on your computer that helps us keep you logged in and understand how you use our Services. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies, but some portions of our Service may not function properly without them.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • To communicate with you, including sending service-related announcements.
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers who need access to your information to carry out work on our behalf (e.g., website hosting).
  • As Required by Law: We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law or in response to a valid request from a law enforcement or government authority.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, since no electronic transmission or storage of data is ever completely secure, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Data Rights and Choices

You have rights over your personal information.

  • Access and Update: You can review and update your account information through your account settings.
  • Data Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of the personal information we hold about you. To request data deletion, please email us at privacy@wrestling.systems. We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws and Facebook Platform Policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@wrestling.systems.
